About AksorAi

AksorAi is a university research project from the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), created by students from the School of Digital Technologies for the ITM-360 Artificial Intelligence course.

The project focuses on using deep learning, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to recognize handwritten text from registration forms and automatically convert it into structured digital data.
